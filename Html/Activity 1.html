Daniel's Recipe for the Best Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 500g ground beef or pork
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese (optional, for topping)
  • Water (for boiling pasta)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pasta
    • Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
    • Add salt to the water for flavor.
    • Cook spaghetti for 8-12 mins or as directed.
    • Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the Sauce
    • Saut√© onions in olive oil until translucent.
    • Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
    • Brown the meat and break into pieces.
    • Add tomato sauce, paste, and crushed tomatoes.
    • Season with herbs, salt, pepper, and optional sugar.
    • Simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Combine and Serve
    • Mix the cooked pasta with the sauce.
    • Serve with grated cheese if desired.
