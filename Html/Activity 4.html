About Me

Daniel Sanchez Deita was born in January 22 2004 at Ipil Doctors Hospital. His mothers name is Josephine Sanchez Deita and his fathers name is Danilo Villacorteza Deita. He has 4 siblings named Juvany Sanchez Deita his eldest brother, Dan Joseph Sanchez Deita his second eldest brother, Daphne Sanchez Deita his only sister and Joefed Sanchez Deita his third elder brother. Daniel and his family lives in a simple house in Purok 4, Baga, Naga Zamboanga Sibugay.


Daniel loves to be at home and spend his time in his room doing his hobby. He loves to draw anime, cartoon, buildings or even a real faces of people in his sketchbook. He started drawing when he was in his Elementary. He joined poster making contest in every school events and also got a consolation prizes. He continued his Junior High-School in Baga National High-School and also continue to improved his skills in arts. After graduating in Junior High School he enrolled in Medina College Ipil to continue his Senior High-School and he took Humss strand. He completed his Senior High School on S.Y. 2022-2023.


Daniel started his college journey by enrolling in Zamboanga Peninsula Polytechnic University Kabasalan Campus. But later on he decided to stop his college and take his time to improve his self. A year later he enrolled in Western Mindanao State University Ipil Campus where he take Associate in Computer Technology (ACT). He met new people, friends, mentors that he know that will help him learn new things and grow as a person.
